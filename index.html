Where traditional VIP relocation falls short

We address the fundamental gaps that create complexity and frustration for executives and their families.

01

Fragmented experience

Most moves rely on a patchwork of agents and vendors with no single owner of the full outcome. The result is duplication, email chains, and unclear accountability.

02

Low transparency & control

Families want real-time visibility into what’s done, blocked, and waiting on decisions — not PDFs. They also need access to options that live off-market and inside trusted networks.

03

Too much time on you

The hidden cost isn’t money — it’s attention. We reduce meetings, decisions, and coordination burden so you can keep leading.
